DeerCamp Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

DeerCamp is built to preserve memories, stories, and traditions in a private, respectful way. We take privacy seriously and collect only what is necessary to provide the app’s core functionality.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Encrypted authentication credentials (managed by Firebase Authentication)

This information is used solely to identify you and secure access to your private memories.

User-Generated Content

When you use DeerCamp, you may create and store:

  • Memory titles
  • Written notes
  • Dates and timestamps
  • Optional photos
  • Optional voice recordings (when enabled)

All memories are private by default and visible only to the signed-in user, unless future sharing features are explicitly enabled by the user.

Device & App Data

We automatically collect limited technical data required for app operation, such as device type, operating system, app version, and crash logs.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Store and retrieve your memories
  • Sync data across devices
  • Improve app reliability and performance

DeerCamp does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioral tracking.

3. Data Storage & Security

DeerCamp uses trusted third-party services to securely store data:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage

Industry-standard security measures are used to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

DeerCamp does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data is shared only with service providers required to operate the app or if required by law.

5. Permissions

DeerCamp may request the following permissions:

  • Photos / Media – to attach images to memories
  • Microphone – for optional voice memories
  • Internet access – to securely sync data

6. User Control & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may edit or delete memories at any time, sign out of the app, or request account deletion.

Account deletion removes associated data from our systems.

7. Children’s Privacy

DeerCamp is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect app improvements or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact:

Founder Promise
DeerCamp exists to preserve camp memories with respect, privacy, and long-term access. Your memories remain yours — protected, private, and never exploited.
