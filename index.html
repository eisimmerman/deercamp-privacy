Privacy Policy for DeerCamp

Last updated: January 23, 2026

Overview

DeerCamp is built to help hunters preserve and relive their deer camp memories. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains what information DeerCamp collects, how it’s used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information (such as email address) used to sign in and secure your account
  • User-generated content, including text memories, photos, and voice recordings you choose to create

Information collected automatically

  • Basic app usage data needed to operate the service securely (such as authentication status)

DeerCamp does not collect location data, contacts, advertising identifiers, or tracking data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide DeerCamp’s core features
  • Store and display your memories
  • Sync your content across devices
  • Maintain account security and reliability

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

Sharing of Information

DeerCamp does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your memories are private unless you choose to share them within a Camp experience (if available).

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit
  • Access to data is restricted to necessary systems
  • Safeguards are in place to reduce unauthorized access

No system is 100% secure, but we are committed to protecting your data responsibly.

Your Choices and Control

You are always in control of your memories.

  • You can view, edit, or delete your memories within the app
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • You can stop using DeerCamp at any time

Founder Promise: DeerCamp will never block access to your memories. What you preserve here is yours—now and forever.

Children’s Privacy

DeerCamp is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as DeerCamp evolves. If changes are made, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised.
